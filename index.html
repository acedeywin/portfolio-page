About Me

Stephen Okpalaononuju is a Research Assistant at the Macauley Institute for Research. He holds a Bsc. in Psychology from Nnamdi Azikiwe University, Awka, and has undergone a few training in technology and other programs to satisfy his curiosity and self development. Prior to his engagement with the Institute, he was involved in Training/Research & Development in a technology firm where he brought innovative ideas and researched on contemporary technologies that helped develop the organization.

Stephen is a passionate and an ambitious fellow with a penchant for information technology. His skill set covers, but not limited to project planning/management, general administrative duties and people management, front-end web development/design, etc, with keen interests in Human-Computer interactions, Research & Development, and Program/Project management. He also likes to read, exercise, surf the internet for helpful information and really enjoys listening to good music.
